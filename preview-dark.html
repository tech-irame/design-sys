Q1 2026 · Operational Risk Assessment

Three controls failed the quarter — none of them fatal.

Across fourteen business units and 312 tested controls, operational risk closed Q1 2026 at a score of 78, up from 66 in Q4 2025. The improvement is meaningful but uneven: IT General Controls and Third-Party Risk both advanced materially, while Change Management regressed slightly on the back of two emergency deployments in late March.

The three failed controls are routine remediations, not structural breakdowns. Privileged access reviews (IT-GEN-04) slipped by forty-seven days while the access governance team absorbed an unplanned head-count change. Backup restoration testing (IT-GEN-11) fell four days past SLA, a symptom of the annual calendar rather than of a testing failure. Change management (IT-GEN-07) is the only finding that warrants policy attention.
